Research


My research interests include metaphysics, ethics, and philosophy of religion. My dissertation was a defense of a relational view of time. Currently my metaphysics projects include work on time as well as grounding. In philosophy of religion I am considering how the view I develop in my dissertation affects our view of divine eternity. For more details on these projects, please visit my research page. If you would like a copy of a paper that I am working on, please send me an email.
